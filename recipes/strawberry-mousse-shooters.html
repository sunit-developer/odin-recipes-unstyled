Strawberry Mousse Shooters

Description

Indulge in these delightful strawberry mousse shooters, a perfect treat for any special occasion or a sweet end to your meal. This elegant dessert features a luscious, creamy mousse made from fresh strawberries, blended into a smooth, velvety texture that melts in your mouth. Served in clear glasses, the vibrant pink mousse is beautifully garnished with whole strawberries and a touch of mint, creating a visually stunning presentation.

Ideal for both formal gatherings and casual get-togethers, these strawberry mousse shooters offer a refreshing and sophisticated flavor profile. The recipe is easy to follow and can be prepared ahead of time, making it a convenient choice for entertaining. Each spoonful delivers a burst of strawberry flavor and a hint of sweetness, providing a light yet indulgent finish to your dining experience.

Ingredients

  • 10 ounces fresh strawberries - trimmed, rinsed, and quartered
  • 3 tablespoons sugar
  • 3/4 cup heavy cream
  • 1/4 cup confectioner's sugar
  • 2 tablespoons strawberry preserves
  • 1/2 teaspoon almond extract
  • Fresh mint and extra chopped strawberries for garnish (optional)

Steps

  1. Add strawberries and sugar to a blender, process to a fine puree, and set aside.
  2. Beat heavy whipping cream in a chilled bowl using chilled beaters until frothy. Add confectioners sugar, strawberry preserves, and almond extract, and continue to beat until stiff peaks form, about 1 to 2 minutes.
  3. Add the strawberry puree equally to the shooter glasses, about a quarter to a third of the way up. Spoon or pipe the mousse on top of the puree, and garnish with fresh mint and strawberries, if using. Move to the fridge to chill.
